# KOL Engagement Sequence

For leads sourced from `kol-engager-icp`. These leads engaged with a Key Opinion Leader's content — they follow industry voices and care about the topic. The KOL is social proof you can reference (they respect this person's content).

**Available personalization variables:**
- `{first_name}`, `{last_name}`, `{company}`, `{title}`, `{industry}`
- `{kol_name}` — the KOL whose post they engaged with
- `{post_topic}` — what the KOL's post was about
- `{engagement_type}` — "commented" or "reacted"
- `{comment_snippet}` — their comment text (if commenter)

---

## Step 1: Connection Request (300 chars max)

### Variant A — KOL-referenced
```
{first_name} — saw you on {kol_name}'s post about {post_topic}. Clearly thinking about this stuff. We're deep in the same space. Let's connect.
```

### Variant B — Topic-first
```
Hey {first_name}, {post_topic} seems to be on everyone's mind in {industry} right now. Would enjoy connecting — we have an interesting angle on this.
```

### Variant C — For commenters
```
{first_name} — your comment on {kol_name}'s post was spot on. We're seeing the same thing. Would be great to connect and compare notes.
```

---

## Step 2: Follow-up 1 — Build on the KOL's Point (Day 3)

### Variant A — Extend the conversation
```
Thanks for connecting, {first_name}. {kol_name}'s post about {post_topic} sparked a lot of good discussion.

One thing that didn't come up: [your unique insight that builds on or challenges the KOL's point]. We've been seeing this play out with {industry} companies specifically.

Is this something you're navigating at {company}?
```

### Variant B — Add data to the discussion
```
Appreciate the connection. Since you were following {kol_name}'s take on {post_topic} — here's a data point that might interest you:

[Specific stat or finding that adds to the conversation]

Curious how that maps to what you're seeing at {company}.
```

### Variant C — For commenters
```
Thanks for connecting. Your comment about {post_topic} — "{comment_snippet}" — is exactly what we hear from {industry} operators.

The gap between what thought leaders like {kol_name} discuss and what actually works on the ground is real. We've been bridging that gap for companies like {company}. Want to hear what's working?
```

---

## Step 3: Follow-up 2 — Social Proof (Day 7)

### Variant A — Results framing
```
{first_name}, following up with something tangible. A {industry} company dealing with the same {post_topic} challenge {kol_name} was talking about — they found a way to [specific result].

Happy to share the approach. Takes about 15 minutes to walk through.
```

### Variant B — Peer validation
```
Fun stat: I've connected with 20+ people from that {kol_name} thread. Almost all of them flagged {post_topic} as a top-3 priority this quarter.

The ones making real progress have one thing in common: [specific insight]. If {company} wants a shortcut to what's working, I can share.
```

---

## Step 4: Follow-up 3 — Breakup (Day 14)

### Variant A
```
{first_name} — not going to keep pinging you on this. If {post_topic} stays a priority for {company}, I'm here. If not, enjoy the connection.
```

### Variant B
```
Last one from me. If you ever want to go deeper on what {kol_name} was talking about — from the implementation side — I've got some good stuff to share. Door's open.
```

---

## Step 5: InMail — Standalone (Day 7, if connection not accepted)

### Subject (200 chars max)
**Variant A:** `Loved {kol_name}'s post on {post_topic} — relevant to {company}`
**Variant B:** `{post_topic}: what {kol_name} didn't mention`
**Variant C:** `{first_name}, building on the {post_topic} conversation`

### Body (1,900 chars max)

**Variant A:**
```
Hi {first_name},

I noticed you engaging with {kol_name}'s post about {post_topic}. It was a great piece — clearly hit a nerve in the {industry} space.

I wanted to reach out because we've been working on the practical side of what {kol_name} was describing. While the post laid out the problem well, the implementation side is where most teams get stuck.

Here's what we've learned working with {industry} companies:
- [Specific insight #1 that builds on the KOL's content]
- [Specific insight #2 — something the KOL's post didn't cover]
- [Specific result a customer achieved]

If {company} is thinking about how to act on this, a quick conversation might save you some trial and error. We've already mapped out the common pitfalls.

Worth a 15-minute chat?

Best,
[Sender name]
```

**Variant B — For commenters:**
```
Hi {first_name},

Your comment on {kol_name}'s {post_topic} post caught my attention: "{comment_snippet}"

You're right — and it's a bigger issue than most people realize. We've been working with {industry} teams on this exact problem, and the pattern is clear: companies that address {post_topic} proactively save [X]% in [cost/time/effort] compared to those that wait.

The tricky part is knowing where to start. Most of the advice out there (including some of what KOLs say) is directionally correct but misses the implementation details.

If you're working through this at {company}, I'd be happy to share what's actually working on the ground — not theory, but results from companies in your space.

Open to a quick conversation?

Best,
[Sender name]
```
